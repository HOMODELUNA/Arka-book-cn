Mailcat

One day, Koyoli who lives in Kateej met a cat. She named him Umi.
She combed him and let him go. Later, he came back. There was a letter on his collar.
It said "Thanks for brushing. We call him Momon in our family."
The girl who wrote the letter was Hinata. Koyoli wrote back to her and put the letter on his collar. This is how their mysterious correspondence began.
