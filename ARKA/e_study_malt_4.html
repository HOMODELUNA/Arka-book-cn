Caser

Casers are prepositions. Clauses, phrases and words can appear after them.
The caser which means the subject (sol) and one which means the object (yul) can be omitted. But they cannot be omitted if the sentence is inverted. You can't omit "sol" or "yul" if the subject or the object is emphasized.
