Leading the Way

xante? tea ti til xafl ento xax xe keom.
ilpasso aata. ti ke van am anmia?
lanskern antisee. an lunas it ilkea anne...
son konoote milf antisee. ke lax kon to anmia?
sox az lop et xilf xalt alka. an en lunas atu kon amo anne.
le naf et lopn e konoote mel. see mir koa a gaavlop fol 4 lopn.
miseeretis!
ti sak sil tu askel xi kend i lop. alva.

Excuse me. Could you lead the way?
OK. Where do you want to go?
To Lanskern Museum. I'm from Ilkea.
It's on Konoote Milf Avenue. How do you go?
By train or bus because I haven't driven here.
The stairs overthere is to the Konoote Mel Station. The museum is four stations away from here.
Thank you.
You'll find it after you get off the train. Take care.

OK. With who?

Where are we going to go next summer vacation?
How about going south and swim in Kateej? We can see Rang Bridge too.

You hate it hot? So Lake Ealis in Alhadir is no good.
I like somewhere cool. Hot spring in north-east, Belfe. Or magic ruins in north-west, Alsia.
The latter one is good. Let's see Mistia there.
