"ant" is "my" and "mine." You only have to remember 7 pronouns in red.
tu et ant = This is mine.
tu et miik ant = This is my apple. cf. Pronoun 5

tu et vait tuul = This is its component.

You have to add -es to make plural possessive pronouns; "antes" (our) and "tiiles" (your).

You can get possessive pronouns 2 by adding -(e)t at the end of original pronouns.
These pronouns are put after nouns.
You should be careful about "vitte."
Here're some examples.

tu et melk neet? = Whose watch is this?
le et vaik totalet a. = That is a component of whatever.
tu et rob welet? tu az le? = To which does this cap belong? To this? Or to that?
