And & Or

There are conjunction, inclusive disjunction and exclusive disjunction in Arka.
They are "o," "az" and "ul."

an siina aples o miik. = I like apricots and apples.

"az" means exclusive disjunction.
aples az miik = an apricot or an apple

"ul" means inclusive disjunction.
aples ul miik = an apricot and/or an apple.

Or Other

aples az miik az alt = an apricot or an apple or other
aples ul miik ul alt = an apricot and/or an apple and/or or other

Whether

You can mean whether with "az."
non en ser nan siina la az. = I don't know whether I like him or not.
