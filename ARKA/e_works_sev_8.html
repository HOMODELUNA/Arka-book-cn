Cacodemonomania

One day, Kagra Trikol, a 16-year-old high school boy, witnessed a death-leap.
After that, he came to be able to see demons.
He took counsel from Hinoto Yuuan, a classmate and a daughter of a shrine.

On the other hand, he found out Kasmi Sarbelia's secret by chance. She is a fellow pupil and loves math.
She ordered him not to give the secret away in a threatening tone. But what he saw was a demon inside her.
