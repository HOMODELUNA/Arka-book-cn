Arka in Manga


Hi, everybody.
Are you getting used to how to read the dictionary?
Today, we're going to read sentences of Arka with Diaklel.



Wow, I'm excited.
What are we going to read? I know the grammar of Arka and how to use the dictionary, but I'm not sure I can read a difficult novel written in Arka.
Plus, I don't want to read boring sentences in coursebooks.



OK, then I'll use this.




Oh, a four-frame comic! There are four-frame comics in your world, too.



There are four characters in this manga. They are the students of Arna University. The name of the manga is "The Diary of a Cat"
Four-frame comics are easy to read because they have introduction, development, turn and conclusion. Thanks to the drawings, you can know their culture and gesture.
I'll explain the four-frame comic on the right one by one.



By the way, Lein and I are also the students of Arna University.
It's called a university, but we'd be a high school student in the USA if the school were in Earth.
Lein is a student of the Ridia House, while I am of the Raldura House. I wonder they are of which House.



I believe they are of Cat House :)
