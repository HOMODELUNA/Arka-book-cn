Lunar Letter

The Lunar letters are the earliest letters in Kaldia. They are ideographs.
Arbazard now uses the Lunar alphabet, but it once used the Lunar letters.
Some of the letters are still used in Arbazard.
cf. Lunar Letter, Libra Tone and Etymology of Lunar Letter.
