Xistina

Mel calendar is used in Arbazard.
A year has 365 days (366 days in a leap year).

Each day has its own traditional color.
The colors are called Xistina. cf. xistina.

Here is the chart of xistina.

You can make a photo book by using Xistina like below.
Each day has its own word. The words are related to the colors.
For example, stark white means honesty.

kaiwata on ruj rav
t'anjur on the day of teems
