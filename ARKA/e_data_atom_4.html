Mana

Brief Overview

Mana is letters made in Altia (Nagi). Earth has kanji while Atolas (or Kaldia) has mana.
cf. mana(2).
The letter below means a bow.

A bow (van) in Kozue (cherry blossom)


A bow (van) in Yuki (strawberry)

Here are school seals of Felishia University. The mana in the latter seal means "the lunar bow."
