And it seems you have to put "la" in front of nouns when it's used as an adjective.
Most adjectives come after nouns in Arka, so "la" is a bit strange.



And you have to put "tu (this)," "le (that)" and "lu (this person)" before nouns when you use them.
"tu miik" means "this apple," while "le galt" does "that gate." And "lu fian" makes "this girl."
You have to use "lu" or "la" when referring to living things.
